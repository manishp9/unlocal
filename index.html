Tequila Sunrise Twist

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz tequila
  • 4 oz orange juice
  • 1/2 oz grenadine
  • 1/2 oz lime juice
  • Ice
  • Orange slice and maraschino
    cherry for garnis

Directions:

  • Fill a tall glass with ice.
  • Pour in the tequila and orange juice.
  • Stir gently to combine.
  • Slowly pour the grenadine over the back of a spoon so it sinks to the bottom and creates a sunrise effect.
  • Add a splash of lime juice for a zesty twist.
  • Garnish with an orange slice and a maraschino cherry.
